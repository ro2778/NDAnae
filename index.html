What's New in v1.1
  • Case Logger — anaesthetic logbook with rota-integrated theatre picker, RCoA-compatible fields, and export to .logbook, Excel, CSV, and PDF
  • Activities on Daily Rota — Acute Pain, Persistent Pain, Pre-op Clinic, Obs Pre-op, and PICC Lines now shown when staffed
v1.0
  • Daily Rota — auto-scraped from CLW, theatres and on-call staffing
  • Useful Information — bleeps, extensions, and door codes
  • WATCh Drug Calculator — paediatric emergency drug doses
  • TEG6 Guideline — quick-reference card for TEG-guided transfusion
  • TEG6 Audit — data collection form with email submission
  • RA Atlas — regional anaesthesia nerve block atlas
